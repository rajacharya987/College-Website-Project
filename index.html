Featured Courses

Explore our diverse range of programs designed to prepare students for successful careers and advanced studies.

BSc.CSIT

This program offers a comprehensive education in computer science and IT, covering programming, systems analysis, and software development. Students gain practical skills and theoretical knowledge for careers in tech industries.

Duration: 4 Years | Timing: 6:30 am - 12 pm

BBS

This undergraduate program covers essential business disciplines such as accounting, finance, marketing, and management, providing a solid foundation for various roles in the business sector.

Duration: 4 Years | Timing: 6:30 am - 10:30 am

Engineering

This program encompasses civil and computer engineering principles, preparing students for careers in construction, infrastructure development, IT support, and software development.

Duration: 3 Years | Timing: 6:30 am - 12 pm

Science

This pre-university course provides foundational knowledge in physics, chemistry, biology, and mathematics, preparing students for higher education in science and technology fields.

Duration: 2 Years | Timing: 10:45 am - 5 pm

Management

This course covers fundamental principles of business and management, including finance, marketing, and organizational behavior, equipping graduates for various roles in business administration.

Duration: 2 Years | Timing: 6:30 am - 10:30 am

Hotel Management

This program focuses on the hospitality industry, teaching hotel operations, food and beverage management, and customer service, preparing students for careers in hospitality management.

Duration: 2 Years | Timing: 6:30 am - 10:30 am
